\section{Overview}
% ============================================================================

This report summarizes findings from the Aggregator surveys conducted across three waves: Baseline, Midline, and Endline. The data was collected as part of the Rwanda Feeder Roads Trucker Experiment.

\subsection{Survey Waves}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Baseline}: Initial household enumeration
    \item \textbf{Midline}: Follow-up survey at intervention midpoint
    \item \textbf{Endline}: Final survey post-intervention
\end{itemize}

\section{Conclusions}
% ============================================================================

This survey provides comprehensive coverage of aggregator business operations in the Rwanda Feeder Roads Trucker Experiment. Key findings include:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Strong panel retention across three waves (89.6\% for all three waves)
    \item Majority of aggregators are individual owners (82.1\%)
    \item Maize and beans are primary crops handled (>60\% of aggregators)
    \item Active participation in seasonal markets (80--84\%)
    \item Growing investment in vehicles and transportation infrastructure over time
    \item High reliance on local market information (90.8\%)
\end{enumerate}

These results form a solid foundation for analysis of intervention impacts on aggregator business performance and market integration.
